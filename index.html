About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher in the vLAR Group at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, working with Prof. Bo Yang. My research focuses on physics learning, 3D vision, and world model. I am interested in building models that can reconstruct, understand, and predict the physical dynamics of 3D scenes from visual observations.

I received my Ph.D. from The Hong Kong Polytechnic University in January 2026. Before that, I obtained my M.S. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Northeastern University, and my B.Econ. in Finance from Guanghua School of Management, Peking University.
